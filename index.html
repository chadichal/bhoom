  • Rose

    • A rose is a woody perennial flowering plant from the Rosa genus, known for its beautiful and often fragrant flowers that come in many colors and varieties.
    With hundreds of species and thousands of cultivars, roses are grown worldwide for their ornamental value in gardens and bouquets, and also have uses in cosmetics, food, and medicine.
    Most have stems with thorns and can grow as shrubs, climbers, or ground cover
  • lily

    • "Lilly" can refer to Eli Lilly and Company, a global pharmaceutical corporation that creates medicines, or to the lily, a genus of tall, perennial flowering plants with many species and a variety of colors and symbolic meanings.
    Eli Lilly is known for its history of developing medications and its research and development efforts, while the lily flower is known for its ornamental value, broad symbolism (such as purity and nobility), and distinct trumpet-shaped flowers.
